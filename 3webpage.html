Just a few things about Naruto

Naruto is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Masashi Kishimoto. It tells the story of Naruto Uzumaki, a young ninja who seeks recognition from his peers and dreams of becoming the Hokage, the leader of his village.

The word naruto refers to a powerful whirlpool formed by the meeting of two opposing tidal currents in this specific strait. This natural phenomenon has long captured the imagination of the Japanese people, leading to its association with the name Naruto.

  • The ninja in The hidden Leaf Village wear a swirl symbol on their ninja uniforms. This swirl is a symbol of the Uzumaki clan that Naruto is descended from.
  • Uzumaki means whirlpool or maelstrom.
